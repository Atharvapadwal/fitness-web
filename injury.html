Injury Prevention & Rehab Guide

Common Injuries & How to Prevent Them

  • Lower Back Pain: Strengthen core muscles, maintain proper posture, and avoid heavy lifting with poor form.
  • Knee Pain: Focus on leg muscle balance, use proper footwear, and avoid excessive impact exercises.
  • Shoulder Injuries: Strengthen rotator cuff muscles, avoid overuse, and warm up properly before workouts.
  • Wrist & Elbow Pain: Stretch wrists, avoid excessive strain from repetitive movements, and maintain proper grip technique.
  • Ankle Sprains: Strengthen ankle muscles, wear supportive shoes, and practice balance exercises.
  • Neck Strain: Maintain proper posture, stretch neck muscles, and avoid prolonged screen time without breaks.
  • Hip Flexor Strain: Strengthen hip flexor muscles and avoid sitting for long periods without stretching.
  • Hamstring Pull: Warm up properly before workouts, stretch hamstrings, and avoid sudden, intense movements.

Recovery & Rehabilitation

  • RICE Method: Rest, Ice, Compression, Elevation for immediate injury management.
  • Stretching & Mobility Work: Helps restore flexibility and reduce stiffness post-injury.
  • Strength Training: Gradual reintroduction of weight training for muscle recovery.
  • Massage & Foam Rolling: Improves blood circulation and reduces muscle tightness.
  • Consult a Physiotherapist: For severe injuries, professional guidance is recommended.
  • Hydration & Nutrition: Proper hydration and a balanced diet help in faster recovery.
  • Mind-Body Techniques: Meditation and breathing exercises can help manage pain and stress.
  • Adequate Sleep: Ensure 7-9 hours of sleep per night to aid in recovery and muscle repair.

Pre-Workout Warm-Up & Post-Workout Cool-Down

  • Warm-Up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio, followed by dynamic stretches.
  • Cool-Down: Static stretching and breathing exercises to relax muscles.
  • Foam Rolling: Helps release muscle tension and improve flexibility.
  • Gradual Progression: Avoid sudden intensity spikes in workouts to prevent injuries.
  • Hydrate: Drink water before, during, and after workouts to maintain hydration levels.
